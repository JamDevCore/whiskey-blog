        # Glenmorangie 18 Year Old Extremely Rare Review

**Brand:** Glenmorangie  
**Age:** 18 Years  
**ABV:** 43%  
**Price:** $111.77

Whisky enthusiasts, gather 'round as we delve into the world of Glenmorangie 18 Year Old Extremely Rare, a dram that promises a journey through complexity and refinement. This Highland single malt Scotch whisky is a testament to the art of ageing and the craft of whisky making.

### First Impressions

Upon first glance, the Glenmorangie 18 Year Old exudes a certain elegance, with packaging that hints at the luxurious experience within. Its golden amber hue is inviting, suggesting a richness that beckons a closer look—or better yet, a taste.

### The Flavor Journey

A gentle swirl of the glass releases an orchestra of aromas. There's a delightful dance of floral notes mixed with ripe fruits and a whisper of honey. The first sip reveals a velvety texture that coats the palate, where the initial sweetness of dried fruits and malt is elegantly balanced with a subtle oakiness. This whisky isn't a punch in the face; it's more of a warm, lingering embrace.

As the flavors develop, there's a delightful surprise of nuttiness and a hint of spice that tickles the tongue, leading to a long and satisfying finish. It's complex without being overwhelming, a trait that enthusiasts and newcomers will both appreciate.

### Value for Money

At just over $111, the Glenmorangie 18 Year Old sits in a competitive price bracket. It's not the most accessible price point for a casual sipper, but for an 18-year-old single malt of this quality, it's reasonably priced. You're paying for the years of maturation and the craftsmanship that goes into each bottle. In terms of value, it's a solid purchase for those looking to indulge in a mature whisky without breaking the bank.

### Comparisons and Context

In the realm of 18-year-old Scotch whiskies, the Glenmorangie 18 Year Old Extremely Rare holds its own. It's smoother and less peated compared to an 18-year-old from Islay, like the Laphroaig 18 Year Old, and offers a different experience than the sherry-rich Macallan 18. It's a middle-ground option for those who appreciate a well-rounded, non-sherried Scotch.

### The Brand and Its Legacy

Glenmorangie, hailing from the Scottish Highlands, has a reputation for creating approachable, high-quality single malts. Their use of the tallest stills in Scotland is said to contribute to the light, pure character of their whiskies. The 18 Year Old Extremely Rare is a shining example of their dedication to excellence and innovation.

### Conclusion

The Glenmorangie 18 Year Old Extremely Rare is a whisky that tells a story—one of patience, expertise, and the beauty of age. It's a whisky that doesn't shout but rather speaks with a confident, refined voice. It's a celebration of subtlety and balance that can be enjoyed by both seasoned aficionados and curious explorers of the whisky world.

### Rating: 4.5/5

**Summary:** The Glenmorangie 18 Year Old Extremely Rare is a beautifully balanced and smooth whisky with a depth that justifies its price. It's not quite the pinnacle of perfection, but it's undeniably close, offering a luxurious experience that's hard to fault. Whether you're expanding your collection or treating yourself to a special dram, this whisky is a choice that's likely to leave you with a lasting impression of satisfaction.
