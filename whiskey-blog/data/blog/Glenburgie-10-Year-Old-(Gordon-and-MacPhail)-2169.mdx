        # Glenburgie 10 Year Old (Gordon and MacPhail) Review

**Whisky Name:** Glenburgie 10 Year Old  
**Brand:** Glenburgie  
**Price:** £31.33  
**ABV:** 40%

## First Impressions

The Glenburgie 10 Year Old, bottled by the reputable Gordon and MacPhail, presents itself in an unassuming manner. The bottle design is straightforward, seemingly allowing the spirit within to speak for itself. Upon uncorking the bottle, you're greeted with a gentle bouquet of aromas that hint at the experience to come.

## Flavour Profile

Taking the first sip, the Glenburgie 10 Year Old reveals a smooth and mellow character. The palate is graced with notes of ripe orchard fruits, a touch of honey sweetness, and a whisper of vanilla. The oak influence is subtle, providing a soft backdrop rather than dominating the profile. The finish is clean, with a mild spice that lingers just long enough to invite another sip.

For those who appreciate a whisky that isn't overbearing, this Glenburgie offers a pleasurable and easy drinking experience. It's not a flavor bomb, but rather a study in restraint and balance.

## Value for Money

At just over £30, this whisky represents a solid value for a 10-year-old single malt. It's an accessible entry point for those new to the world of whisky, as well as a pleasant everyday dram for the seasoned enthusiast looking for something uncomplicated yet satisfying.

## Comparisons and Brand Info

Glenburgie itself might not be as widely recognized as some of its Speyside cousins, but it's a distillery that contributes significantly to blended Scotch whiskies. This expression by Gordon and MacPhail offers a rare glimpse into the distillery's own character.

If you're exploring similar whiskies, you may look towards the likes of Glen Moray or BenRiach's 10-year-old expressions, which also offer fruit-forward profiles and approachability, often at a comparable price point.

## Summary

The Glenburgie 10 Year Old is a gentle Speyside single malt that delivers a straightforward, no-frills whisky experience. It's the kind of dram that doesn't demand your full attention, making it perfect for a relaxed evening.

**Rating:** 3.5/5

**Summary:** A solid, budget-friendly single malt that provides a smooth and enjoyable sipping experience, perfect for both newcomers and those looking for a dependable everyday dram.
